<p><p>The handheld welding torch market primarily consists of two key types: MIG/MAG welding torches and TIG welding torches. MIG/MAG welding torches are designed for metal inert gas or metal active gas welding, offering efficiency and versatility for thicker materials. In contrast, TIG welding torches provide precise control and are ideal for welding thinner metals, producing high-quality welds. Both types cater to different welding applications, contributing to their distinct market segments within the broader welding industry.</p></p>
