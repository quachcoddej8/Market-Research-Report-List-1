<p><p>A Raman amplifier is a device used in optical communication systems to boost the power of an optical signal. It operates based on the principle of stimulated Raman scattering, where the signal interacts with a pump laser in a medium, typically optical fiber. This interaction amplifies the signal without the need for direct electrical intervention, making Raman amplifiers highly efficient for long-distance communication.</p><p>The Raman amplifier market is witnessing significant growth, driven by an increasing demand for high-capacity bandwidth and the proliferation of data centers.
