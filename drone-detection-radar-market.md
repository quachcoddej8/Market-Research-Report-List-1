<p><ul><li>FMCW Radar</li><li>Pulse-Doppler Radar</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The drone detection radar market includes several technologies, primarily FMCW (Frequency Modulated Continuous Wave) radar and Pulse-Doppler radar. FMCW radar provides continuous frequency modulation, enabling precise distance measurement and detection of drones in various conditions. Pulse-Doppler radar, on the other hand, delivers high accuracy by analyzing the frequency shift of returned signals, distinguishing between stationary and moving targets. Other radar types may include hybrid systems or specialized technologies aimed at enhancing detection capabilities for different environmental scenarios.</p></p>
